Kodak Black completes 90 days of rehab, probation violation lifted



American rapper, Kodak Black’s probation violation has been lifted after successfully completing 90 days of drug rehab. 

The rapper and producer was ordered to a rehab facility a few months back after failing a drug test, a violation of the terms of his supervised release in his federal gun case.

The rapper who is now clean, sober and also turning his attention to his career, had a court hearing to reinstate his supervised release. His lawyer, Bradford Cohen said the judge was happy with Kodak’s progress.

Cohen said Kodak is looking forward to making good music, and is going to try to stay away from people, places and things that would put him in harm’s way, or trigger him to use again.
