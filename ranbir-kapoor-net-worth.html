Indian film actor Ranbir Kapoor has an estimated net worth of $30 million. Born on September 28, 1982 in Mumbai, he belongs to the fourth generation of this eminent Bollywood family. He is the son of both actors, Rishi Kapoor and Neetu Singh. He is also the nephew of actors Randhir, Rajiv and Kunal Kapoor. He is the grandnephew of Shashi Kapoor, Jennifer Kendal, Shammi Kapoor and Geeta Bali who were also actors. And he is the cousin of Kareena Kapoor and Karisma Kapoor.

Ranbir Kapoor began his career in the movie industry as an assistant to the film director Sanjay Leela Bhansali in 2005 during the production of the film Black. He made his acting debut in the film Saawariya. It was not a big hit, but still earned him critical approval.

From 2005 through 2009, he has made another three notable performances in movies including Wake Up Sid, Ajab Prem Ki Ghazab, and Rocket Sing: Saleman of the Year, all of which were financially successful. His acclaimed work on those films led to his winning of the Filmfare Critics Award for Best Actor in 2010.

In 2012, he received another Filmfare Award for Best Actor in the Imtiaz Ali-directed drama Rockstar, as well as for his portrayal of a deaf mute man in Barfi! He is considered one of the most bankable stars in Bollywood.
