Maroon 5 Lands Las Vegas Residency Amid Adam Levines Cheating Scandal

Adam Levine’s personal life may be in disarray, but his career is still thriving. The singer’s band has announced they’re set to headline a new residency at Park MGM in Las Vegas.

According to a press release, the show dates will begin Friday, March 24, 2023. The band will perform 16 shows from March to August. Tickets will be available for purchase by the public on Monday, October 3 at 10a.m. PT.

There will also be a pre-sale beginning on Wednesday, September 28 at 10a.m. PT. Members of MGM Rewards as well as Live Nation and Ticketmaster customers are eligible for the pre-sale tickets, which will end the day before all tickets go on sale.

Maroon 5 shared the news with their fans on their official Instagram page. “M5LV THE RESIDENCY We’re headed back to Vegas!” the caption read. It was posted alongside a neon green photo featuring the band’s Las Vegas logo.

Interestingly, the band turned off comments on the announcement post. In fact, Maroon 5 has disabled comments on their last few IG posts.

Despite being the band’s lead singer, Adam hasn’t made any posts on social media about the residency (although he did change his Instagram profile picture to their Las Vegas logo).

Earlier this month, influencer Sumner Stroh shared a now-viral TikTok accusing the singer of having a year-long affair with her. She shared a series of screenshots featuring messages Adam allegedly sent her.

One screenshot showed Adam acknowledge that his wife, Behati Prinsloo, is pregnant with their third child. He said he “dead serious” wanted to name the baby after Sumner.

Adam has responded to the cheating allegations. Though he denies having an affair, he admits that boundaries were crossed.

“ I used poor judgment in speaking with anyone other than my wife in ANY kind of flirtatious manner,” he said via Instagram. “I did not have an affair, nevertheless, I crossed the line during a regrettable period of my life.”

The musician added, "In certain instances it became inappropriate; I have addressed that and taken proactive steps to remedy this with my family." However, several other women have come forward alleging they had a similar experience with Adam.

Sources say that while Adam’s pregnant wife is “very upset” with him, they’re not considering divorce at the moment, noting that the Victoria’s Secret model is “committed to her family.”
