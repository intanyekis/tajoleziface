The World's Most expensive Bag

The House of Mouawad has just unveiled the Mouawad 1001 Nights Diamond Purse, the world’s most expensive handbag, valued at a staggering $3.8 million. The bespoke handbag has been already recorded by the Guinness Book of World Records as being the most expensive purse ever.

The 1001 Nights Diamond Purse is encrusted with 18 carat gold and features 4517diamonds. The diamonds on this pricy creation weighs to an impressive 381.92 carat.

Designed by Robert Mouawad, this is their second world record level creation, the first being $11 million Sexy Fantasy Bra for Victoria’s secret way back in 2003. Talk about style and taste! Their new creation will be on show at the Doha Jewelry and Watches Exhibition, Qatar.
