Who Is BoyWithUke?

BoyWithUke is the stage name for Charley Yang, an American-Korean singer, musician, and internet personality. He first became well-known on the social media app TikTok with his tune “Two Moons” before becoming well-known for his succeeding songs “Toxic” and “Understand”. Up until October 2023, he remained anonymous. Currently, Mercury Records, a branch of UMG, is his label.

After being introduced to TikTok by his brother in 2020, BoyWithUke started submitting videos there. Until 2021, some of his videos—including a series of videos named “Minute-Long Songs”—performed well on the site but did not initially draw much attention.
