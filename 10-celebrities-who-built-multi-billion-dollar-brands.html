In the entertainment world, many celebrities have leveraged their astounding net worth, fame, and influence to venture into the business world. Their entrepreneurial journeys are as diverse as their talents, spanning various industries from fashion and music to technology and sports. These ventures provide an additional income stream and allow these celebrities to create a lasting legacy beyond their primary careers.

These celebrities have created successful brands and companies, proving they are entertainers and savvy businesspeople.

George Clooney & Rande Gerber - Casamigos Tequila

Casamigos Tequila, a brand co-founded by George Clooney and Rande Gerber, has become a household name in the world of spirits. Launched in 2013, the brand was initially a private collection for the duo and their friends. However, its popularity led to a public launch. In 2017, Diageo, a global leader in beverage alcohol, acquired Casamigos for a staggering $1 billion, marking a significant milestone in the brand's journey, according to E-News.

Magic Johnson - Magic Johnson Enterprises

Earvin ‘Magic’ Johnson, the legendary basketball player, has proven his business acumen with Magic Johnson Enterprises. Established in 1987, this conglomerate has strategically invested in various sectors, including entertainment, real estate, and food services. Johnson's business ventures extend beyond the traditional endorsements and licensing deals that many athletes engage in. His company's estimated net worth of $1 billion is a testament to his entrepreneurial prowess and business acumen.

Mary-Kate & Ashley Olsen - Multiple Brands

Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen, the famous twin actresses, have successfully transitioned from their early acting careers to becoming respected fashion figures. They have built a fashion empire with multiple brands, including The Row, Elizabeth and James, and Olsenboye. The Row, in particular, has gained recognition for its luxury items, winning the CFDA Womenswear Designer of the Year in 2012 and 2015, according to The Hollywood Reporter. Their successful ventures in the fashion industry, which have a combined value of around $1 Billion, have significantly contributed to the twins' combined net worth of approximately $500 million.

Beyoncé & Jay Z - Tidal And Ivy Park

Music power couple Beyoncé and Jay Z have made significant strides in business with their ventures, Tidal, a music streaming service, and Ivy Park, a famous athleisure brand. Tidal, acquired by Jay Z in 2015, is currently valued at $377.5 million and aims to provide a fairer model for artists. According to Forbes, Ivy Park, co-founded by Beyoncé in 2016, promotes body positivity and empowerment and is currently valued at around $450 Million. These ventures have contributed to the couple's combined net worth of over $1 billion.

Jessica Simpson - Jessica Simpson Collection

Jessica Simpson, an American singer, actress, and fashion designer, launched the eponymous brand in 2005 in partnership with Vince Camuto, the co-founder of the footwear company Nine West. The Jessica Simpson Collection is a fashion line of clothing and other items for women and juniors. Besides clothing, the brand includes handbags, sunglasses, accessories, jewelry, shoes, and fragrances. The collection has been a significant success, earning $1 billion in annual sales as of 2014.

Jessica Alba - The Honest Company

Actress Jessica Alba co-founded The Honest Company, which offers eco-friendly baby and beauty products. Since its inception in 2011, the company has gained a loyal customer base, leading to its current valuation of approximately $1.5 billion. Alba's commitment to providing safe and effective products has driven the brand's success, demonstrating her entrepreneurial spirit and dedication to social responsibility.

Kate Hudson - Fabletics

Fabletics is a fitness and athleisure brand co-founded by actress Kate Hudson. Its subscription-based service provides members with personalized athletic wear outfits each month. Fabletics focuses on providing stylish and high-quality activewear at affordable prices. The brand is known for its inclusive sizing options and its commitment to sustainability. Fabletics has gained popularity for its convenient online shopping experience and emphasis on empowering women to lead active and healthy lifestyles. The famous brand currently generates annual revenue of $500 Million and has a valuation of over $1.5 Billion.

Bono - Elevation Partners

Bono, the U2 frontman, demonstrated his business savvy by co-founding Elevation Partners, a private equity firm, in 2004. The firm has strategically invested in technology companies, including Facebook and Yelp. These wise investments have not only contributed to the success of Elevation Partners, which currently has over $2 Billion as assets but have also helped increase Bono's net worth to an estimated $700 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Sean Diddy Combs - Bad Boy Worldwide Entertainment Group

Sean Diddy Combs, the music mogul, has significantly impacted the music industry with his Bad Boy Worldwide Entertainment Group. The company was founded in 1993 and includes a record label and a production company. It has played a pivotal role in shaping the careers of many artists, including Notorious B.I.G., Mase, and Faith Evans. With a rumored net worth of $3.96 billion, Bad Boy Worldwide Entertainment Group reflects Combs' influence in the music industry.

Michael Jordan - Air Jordan

Basketball legend Michael Jordan has significantly impacted the sports footwear industry with Air Jordan, a brand of basketball shoes produced by Nike. Since its launch in 1984, Air Jordan has become synonymous with premium sports footwear. It has become Nike’s highest-selling brand generating over $5.2 Billion in annual revenue with a monumental $10 Billion valuation. The brand's success has contributed significantly to Jordan's net worth of $1.6 billion, demonstrating his influence on and off the basketball court.
