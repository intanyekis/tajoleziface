Paul Walkers Daughter, Meadow Walkers Divorce Just Took A Big Turn

Highlights

  • Meadow Walker and Louis Thornton-Allan have finally reached a divorce agreement after some back-and-forth negotiations.
  • The couple agreed on the division of property and support, hoping the court will finalize their uncontested decision.
  • Meadow shared her love life on social media, including her marriage, but her posts don't mention Thornton-Allan now.

Paul Walker's daughter, Meadow Walker, surprised fans when she announced she intended to divorce Louis Thornton-Allan. Since then, there has been a bit of back-and-forth as the former couple try to negotiate the terms of their split. However, things have recently taken a big turn because Walker and Thornton-Allan reportedly have reached a divorce agreement.

After Walked filed for divorce, citing "irreconcilable differences," she reportedly also wanted to avoid paying spousal support, InTouch Weekly reports. Her reason was that she and Thornton-Allan had previously signed a prenuptial agreement. However, Thornton-Allan had requested spousal support in addition to having his legal fees paid by Walker. The case has now taken a positive turn, as the former couple appear to be seeing eye-to-eye on how to proceed.

What Did Meadow Walker And Her Ex-Husband Agree On?

According to InTouch Weekly, Meadow Walker and Louis Thornton-Allan have reached an agreement that includes "the division of their property and support." The publication obtained court documents from the filing, which stated the couple "agree that this cause may be decided as an uncontested matter."

The court documents also stated that "the parties have a written agreement that will be submitted to the court, or a stipulation for judgment that will be submitted to the court." The former couple may be in an agreement, but InTouch Weekly notes the judge has yet to sign off.

But what went wrong in the first place? Walker and Thornton-Allan married in October 2021, but in December 2023, she ended their relationship, informing fans with a statement shared via Instagram. "After three wonderful years of marriage, we have come to the agreement to amicably separate," they said at the time.

The statement continued, "This is truly a united decision, and we sincerely hope everyone can respect our wishes for privacy. We maintain mutual love and respect for one another and will continue to support each other." Despite this incredibly positive statement, things did not stay like this, as the former couple would disagree on the issue of spousal support.

Why Did Meadow Walker Get Divorced?

Meadow Walker shared a glimpse into her life and her relationship with fans on social media. This included making her relationship official on Instagram in July 2021. At the time, her beau posted a photo of himself and Walker looking incredibly in love. He captioned the post with "Best friend," and People notes that Walker responded in the comments, writing, "My love."

Walker also celebrated their wedding day on Instagram, surrounded by her loved ones. Guests included her late father, Paul Walker's good friend, and co-star Vin Diesel, who walked her down the aisle. He is also her god father. At the time, Meadow posted a picture of the celebration and captioned it with a simple message, "We're married!!!!"
