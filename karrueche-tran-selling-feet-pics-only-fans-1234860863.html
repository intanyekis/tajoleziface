Karrueche Tran Defends Selling Feet Pics On Only Fans

Karrueche Tran recently spoke out after fans discovered that she was selling pictures of her feet on the private subscription site, OnlyFans.

Critics began making claims that the model and actress’ latest venture was due to her “going broke,” to which she set the record straight.

“LOL at all the comments regarding OF,” she wrote in a since-deleted tweet on Tuesday (Mar. 12). “I’m not going broke.. just a smart play. Foot fetish is the number one fetish in the world. There’s nothing sexual that will be on my page. It’s literally my feet and if people are willing to pay, then what’s the problem.”

In another X post, the Claws star said she will be donating a percentage of her OnlyFans revenue to local charities in Los Angeles.

“I have a team that helps with the page, so they’ll be able to eat from this as well,” she wrote. “There’s a stigma around OF but there’s literally chefs, musicians, yoga instructors, etc. on there.”

According to Tran’s account, her subscription fee is $24.99 a month or $52.48 for a three-month bundle and $104.96 for six-months.

Many celebrities have taken to OnlyFans to cash in on their own assets including rappers like Asian Doll, Bhad Babie, and more.

Bhad Bhabie reportedly made over $50 million from her OnlyFans after joining the social media platform after her 18th birthday in April 2021. She reportedly earned more than $1 million dollars in six hours.

According to her Instagram post, Bhabie grossed $52,892,972.42 off her OnlyFans from April 1, 2021 through April 25, 2022, taking home $42,309,719.03 in revenue minus OnlyFans’ cut.
