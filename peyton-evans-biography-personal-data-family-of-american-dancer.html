Peyton Evans Biography, Personal Data, Family of American Dancer

Peyton Evans is a beautiful young dancer popularly known for her amazing moves on the dance floor. Evans started hitting the floor when she was just 18 months old and by the time she turned three she had already started beating older dancers in competitions.

Peyton is blessed with broad dance styles that include lyrical, acrobatic, and jazz. On several occasions, she presented incredible solo jazz performances to the public, winning their admiration and praise. The young dancer can perfectly deliver any dance form at any time, but her favorite is acrobatics, a type of dance that incorporates acrobatic elements with classical dance technique.

Peyton Evans Bio

Nicknamed Peyts, the professional dancer was born to American parents in a town called Tooele, Utah. Her date of birth was February 17, 2009. The American dancer has only one sibling, one brother.

Evans’ best dance skill is acrobatics, mastering the art as a toddler. She did her first training at the ALDC LA dance studio located in California before participating in the JUMP Dance Convention and the America On Stage Dance Competition. Peyton found mainstream success after starring in the sixth season of “Dance Moms,” with her mom.

The Lifetime reality show reportedly showcased all the emotion and drama she experienced at the studio where she began her dance training. The skilled rocker made his first television appearance with his mother as a member of the ALDC mini team. Peyton stole the show not only because she was the youngest dancer, but also because she delivered her best acrobatic skills in the show.

Unfortunately, the professional dancer and her mother decided to leave the show the following season. While their stint lasted on Dance Moms, Evans established a friendly relationship with fellow dancers – Kalani Hilliker and Mackenzie Ziegler. She considers them respectively as her role model and her mentor.

At the Utah Dance Fest held in 2016, Peyton Evans emerged victorious in the solo category and was applauded during the competition. Currently, the Utah native works closely with MöDA Talent Management, a Utah-based talent agency. Previously, she was associated with Prodigy Dance Academy.

The aspiring model/singer has a self-titled YouTube where her mother uploads virtually all of her performances. The channel currently has five thousand subscribers and thousands of views. Peyton Evans is also active on Instagram where she has nearly 400,000 followers. She is also a notable face on TikTok previously known as Musical.ly.

American Dancer’s Family

Peyton Evans family members include her brother and parents. Her parents’ names are Mike Evans and Kerri Evans while Jordan Evans is the name of her only brother. Not much is known about the dancer’s father and brother, as there is no information available about them. However, we have to point out that they are very proud of Peyton and her blossoming career.

Kerri, who also serves as her daughter’s manager, was also part of the Mini Competition Team in the 3rd episode of Dance Moms sixth season, Mini Dancers, Big Drama. Born on June 11, 1984, Kerri has been very supportive of her daughter from her early days in pageantry and dancing.

She has 53.2k followers on her Twitter page, as well as a dancewear line called the Peyton K Collection. During the fifth episode of the sixth season of Dance Moms, Kerri Evans and fellow dance star Tiffanie Olad got into a serious off-screen argument.
