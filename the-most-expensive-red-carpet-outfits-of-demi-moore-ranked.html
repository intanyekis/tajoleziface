Demi Gene Guynes moved around as a child due to her stepfather’s job changes. At 15, she lived in California when she dropped out of high school and took drama lessons while working with Elite Modeling. In 1980, she married musician Freddy Moore and took his last name, which remains unchanged. A year later, she made her film debut in Choices and Parasite before bagging a supporting role in the popular soap opera General Hospital. With the release of St. Elmo’s Fire in 1985, Moore earned a breakthrough that led to multiple high-budget films. She became a red carpet-fixture and one of the highest-paid actresses in the 1980s and 1990s.

Over the last four decades, Demi Moore has worked with several stylists who have helped the actress pave a style of her own. From wearing iconic Spanx shorts at the Academy Awards to bespoke power suits gaining popularity, the actress has evolved her chic looks. Let’s take a look at Demi Moore’s most expensive outfits.

1989 Academy Awards

Demi Moore has always dressed in classic outfits during the 80s and 90s, but nothing has topped her 1989 Oscars look. According to Vogue, the actress wore a bodysuit and dress hybrid that Moore fashioned herself. She wore a metallic floral-print fabric with spandex shorts. While the outfit was deemed in the worst-dressed category, the same hybrid look costs more than $4,500 today.

1997 Golden Globes

The actress arrived with her then-husband Bruce Willis wearing a floral print cap sleeve gown in lilac. The outfit featured a simple neckline with metallic appliques. Her outfit was paired with diamond clip earrings and a bracelet. Her outfit was one of Elle’s Best Golden Globes Outfits Of All Time.

78th Venice International Film Festival

Cut-out gowns have been in fashion since 2021, with many actresses showing their trend-setting outfits for premieres and red carpets. Moore arrived in a sleek white gown with a cut-out pattern, plunging neckline, and exposed midriff from designer Mônot’s collection. She left her long locks loose and accessorized with a bracelet and matching silver earrings.

1992 Academy Awards

Demi Moore looked ethereal when she arrived at the 1993 Oscars in a lilac Versace gown borrowed from the vintage collection. It has been notably called the best Versace looks of all time, said to be priceless for the fashion house. Moore’s gown had a floral bodice and long gloves, and she matched it with a shrug. The actress wore it in honor of the fashion icon who had died a few months before the ceremony.

2019 Met Gala

Demi Moore stunned at the Met Gala as she celebrated the Camp: Notes on a Fashion theme. As noted by Yahoo Movies, her incredible physique was seen in the black plunging Yves Saint Laurent gown custom-made for the actress. A similar gown retails for $6,990 on the fashion house’s website. She had paired the look with dangling diamond earrings and a long silver chain.

2013 Performing Arts Inaugural Gala

The Wallis Annenberg Center for the Performing Arts Inaugural Gala saw a rare attendance from Demi Moore. She had not been in public for six months before making a red-carpet appearance. She arrived in a glitzy navy Ferragamo halter neck gown beaded with sparkling appliques. Moore wore her usual accessories of a bracelet and earrings.

2021 Fashion Awards

Arriving at the Fashion Awards to celebrate her friend and fashion designer Kim Jones, Demi Moore sparkled on the red carpet with her black and white outfit. The Fendi dress got dark in the bottom and was encrusted with embellishments for a sparkling look. She wore black pumps and leather gloves and carried a clutch to finish the look. The dress also had a black mesh train that stole the spotlight and took up most of the platform.

Blind Movie Premiere

Gucci is known for its iconic floral motifs, and the actress channeled the fashion house’s best suit at the 2017 Blind movie’s premiere. As mentioned by US Weekly, she made a statement in the floral suit that featured a matching jacket and trousers. She paired the Fall 2017 outfit with a Gucci clutch and Christian Louboutin Aketi Scallop Ankle-Wrap Pump that retail for $750.

2020 Vanity Fair Oscar Party

Demi Moore arrived with her daughter Rumor Willis for the 2020 Vanity Fair Oscar Party, and the mother-daughter duo made heads turn with their stunning outfits. Demi was dressed in a Yves Saint Laurent classic column dress in black that shimmered and featured a thigh-high split. Her outfit was paired with black stilettos and diamond chandelier earrings.

Indecent Proposal Dress

Demi Moore has given several iconic performances throughout her career, one of the most iconic being her portrayal in the 1993 drama Indecent Proposal. In the film, her character was offered $1 million to spend the night with a man, and during the scene, she was dressed in a stunning black Thierry Mugler gown worth $5,000, as stated by Time. Her gown had a body-hugging silhouette with a cross-body halter neckline, giving a unique outlook.

Demi Moore has continued her fashion reign in the industry with her mighty yet sublime looks. The actress went from wearing delicate gowns to power suits, but her statement jewelry of a bracelet and earrings has remained evergreen. She remains a fixture on the red carpet even today and brings her best fashion foot forward.
