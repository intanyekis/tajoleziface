Kenny DeForest a Comedian, seriously injured after being hit by vehicle in New York

Comedian Kenny DeForest, originally from Springfield and now living in New York, had brain surgery after getting hit by a car on Friday, December 8, 2023.

Kenny DeForest was riding his bike in Brooklyn when a car hit him, causing a serious injury. Emergency treatment was needed, and he had a tough operation at Kings County Hospital, where part of his skull was removed.

Right now, Kenny is stable but taking a lot of medicine. The medical team is impressed with his strength and sees improvement every day. Despite the tough situation, Kenny’s determination has greatly impacted the doctors and nurses caring for him.

Carmen Morales shared the news of his accident on Facebook and asked for help. She mentioned Kenny’s surgery and urged people to donate or spread the word about the fundraising link. Kenny, a talented comedian and actor, had a bad accident while biking, resulting in severe injuries.

As per reports, He’s still conscious and spending most of his time sleeping due to his injuries. Even though he’s not fully awake, Kenny shows strength, giving hope for a full recovery. He’s getting the necessary treatments and is closely watched by specialists. Good wishes come from friends, family, and fans, hoping for a quick recovery.

GoFundMe Fundraiser

Ryan Beck started a GoFundMe page to collect money for Kenny’s treatment. He explained what happened and shared that Kenny is progressing, although the road to recovery is tough. The campaign aimed to raise $150,000, and, $136,519 has been collected. People can still donate to help Kenny during this hard time.

The Kenny family manages the funds, ensuring the money goes toward Kenny’s healing and medical costs. If you want to support Kenny, consider contributing to the fundraiser or telling others about it. Every little bit helps on Kenny’s journey to recovery.
