Who is Sunshine Deia Tutt?

Tutt, born and raised in Brownsville, Texas is a model who also had a brief acting stint. She was engaged to Kattan in 2006 on Christmas Eve at her grandparents' home in Texas, however, it was their marriage that ended just two months after they tied the knot. The couple were married at Yosemite Valley in 2008 and was attended by a close group that included Will Ferrell, Jimmy Fallon, Brooke Shields, and Charlie Sheen. According to Closer Weekly, the comedian had planned for something special.

“Originally I was going to do something very special,” he told People in 2007. “I tried to get Bono of U2 to serenade her. That didn’t happen. Then I asked Justin Timberlake. No answer. After hundreds of phone calls and text messages, I finally ended up asking one of the members of the Hanson group. He didn’t want to get involved.” It was a pity that their marriage didn't last as the divorce was finalized in 2009. Since then, Tutt has been focussing on Sublime Interior Designs, her interior decor company.
