Who is Madilyn Bailey?

Madilyn Bailey is an American singer and songwriter.

She has known for her singles titled “Titanium”, “Radioactive”, “Believe” and “Rude”.

Madilyn Bailey: Birth, Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

Bailey was born to American parents on September 2, 1992, in Boyceville, Wisconsin, The United States of America.

Her nationality is American and belongs to English and Irish ethnicity.

Her father and mother are Greg Wold and Heidi Wold respectively.

There is no more information available about her siblings. Nothing is published about Bailey’s educational background.

Madilyn Bailey: Professional Life, Career

Madilyn Bailey’s first extended play titled “Bad Habit EP” was released on May 2, 2012.

After three years on October 2, 2015, she released her first album titled “Muse Box” which is available in CD and download format. Bailey released her first single titled “Titanium” in 2015.

In the same year (2015), she released her other single titled “Rude”.

In 2016, her two singles titled “Believe” and “Wiser” were released. Her second extended play titled “Wiser EP” was released on July 12, 2016.

She appeared on America’s Got Talent’s 16th season in 2021, performing an original song composed entirely of hate comments that had received over 20 million views on Youtube.
