Adeles Boyfriend Is Hesitant To Get Married Over Prenup

Adele has said she’s open to walking down the aisle again, which has fuelled speculation that she’s gearing up to wed her boyfriend, Rich Paul. But according to a new report, he’s not anxious to tie the knot anytime soon.

Speaking to Radar Online, a source revealed that Adele would love to get hitched, specially while she’s in Las Vegas for her residency. But it appears Rich isn’t looking forward to all the paperwork that will come with officialising their union, including agreeing on a prenup.

“If Adele had her way, they’d be married already. She’s begged Rich to elope countless times and has set her heart on doing it in Las Vegas sooner than later,” the insider said.

“He’s happy with the way things are, for now, wanting to get through the holidays and dealing with formalities, including the prenup,” they continued.

However, they may tie the knot sooner than later. The same source said that Rich’s hesitation has caused Adele to think about pulling the plug on their relationship, which may be the motivation he needs to make a wedding happen.

“In fact, she’s [Adele] at the point where’s ready to say, ‘Marry me or move on!” they continued. “Rich would prefer to take more time. But Adele’s the boss at the end of the day and gets what she wants.”

In September, the singer revealed she’d love to get married again. Adele was previously married to Simon Konecki from 2018 to 2021. The former couple share one child, Angelo, 9.

“Yes, absolutely,” Adele said when asked if she’s open to marriage. But she clarified she’s not engaged just yet.

“I’m not engaged. I just love high-end jewelry, boy!” she said in response to engagement rumors that began circulating after she was seen wearing a massive diamond on her ring finger.

That wasn’t the first time Adele and Rich sparked engagement speculation. The same month, Adele made a post featuring her Emmy, but it was one of her coffee books that caught the attention of her followers.

The book featured the title “The Paul’s,” leading some to believe it was a memento from their wedding. Ultimately, however, it was just rumors.

Adele may hope to get married soon, but she’s busy with her Las Vegas residency, which just re-launched after it was cancelled amid reports of creative differences earlier this year.
