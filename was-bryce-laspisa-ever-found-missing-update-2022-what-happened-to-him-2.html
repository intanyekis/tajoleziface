Was Bryce Laspisa Ever Found? Missing Update What Happened To Him?

Bryce Laspisa was only 19 when he vanished off the Sierra Pelona Mountains. Was he ever found?

The chilling disappearance of Bryce Laspisa has become one of the most discussed conspiracy theories on the internet, with many channels giving their take bout his current whereabouts.

But the truth of the matter remains ingrained in mental illness and substance abuse that led to the downward spiral of an innocent life that could never get recovered.

Was Bryce Laspisa Ever Found? Missing Person Update

Bryce Laspisa was on his way to his parent’s house when his car got found in an unrecognizable state near Castaic Lake.

Surprisingly, there was no trace of a driver as he simply vanished into thin air.

But, there is more to his sudden need to evaporate as he began as a freshman studying graphic and industrial design at Sierra College and moved to the north to Chico.

The first year went smoothly, as he had an honest roommate and average grades and had just found love with fellow enrolee Kim Sly.

His roommate Sean recalled that things took a drastic turn when he became more withdrawn and erratic and even admitted to taking Vyvanse, a drug for ADHD.

Eventually, his concerned girlfriend asked him about his habits which ultimately resulted in a breakup.

It was the breaking point as he refused to take flight time and decided to drive him to ponder about his life.

What Is The Story Behind The Disappearance Of Bryce Laspisa?

The story of teenager Bryce Laspisa is one of the most chilling urban mysteries as he vanished into thin air in August of 2013 when steering home to see his parents.

According to examinations, the 19 years old was on the road around 2 am when he called his mother to inform her that he was pulling off to the side of Interstate 5 in the Sierra Pelona Mountains and would reach home soon.

His parents resided in Laguna Niguel, California, not too far from the site, as his family happily awaited their son’s routine from college.

Little known to them, he would never come back home as even law and authorities are puzzled about his whereabouts.

The journey meant to be a three-hour ride catapulted to half a day of waiting.

When the doorbell finally rang, his mother rushed to the door to welcome her son, but in front were cops asking for information about their son’s car wreck.

Who Was Bryce Laspisa, And Who Were His Parents?

Bryce Laspisa, the son of Michael and Karen Laspisa, was born in Springfield, Illinois, on 30th April 1994, as he was only 19 during his unfortunate disappearance.

As the only child of his living parents, there was not much they would refuse and give importance to his every need.

At a young age, he started showing artistic abilities as his charming personality and easy-going attitude made him people’s favorite. He was an ex-pupil of Naperville Central High School when his newly retired parents decided to move the family to California.
