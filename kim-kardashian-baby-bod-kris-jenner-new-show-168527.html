Kim Kardashian To Reveal Post-Baby Bod on Kris Jenners Talk Show?

Maintaining a low profile since giving birth to daughter North West last month, Kim Kardashian has plans to debut her post-baby body this summer on mom Kris Jenner‘s new daytime talk show. According to Us Weekly, the “Keeping Up With The Kardashians” star is working hard to get her figure back for the big reveal, ensuring that her return to the spotlight is memorable one.

“She wants to show up like Heidi Klum and have everyone marvel at how fast she lost the weight,” a source tells Us Weekly. “She won’t leave the house until she’s thinner.”

After being instructed by doctors to take it slow post-delivery, Kardashian is currently spending time at Kris’ Hidden Hills, Calif. home. While she can’t exercise just yet, Kim has reportedly lost 20 of the 50 pounds she gained during pregnancy. Still, motherhood seems to fit her.

“These past couple of weeks have been filled with the most exciting experiences of my life,” Kanye West’s main squeeze wrote on her blog on Wednesday (July 17). “I’m enjoying this time to fully embrace motherhood and spend time at home with my family. I’ve been reading all of your messages and want to thank you all for your sweet thoughts and best wishes. It truly means everything to me. I am so blessed to have the support of my family and fans in this beautiful moment.”

Even Kris is beaming for her daughter. “I’ve just never seen her this happy,” Jenner told Us Weekly. “She’s just embracing the whole thing, and that just makes me very proud.” —Andrew Asare
