Who is Toby Stephens?

Toby Stephens is an English actor in both television series and movies as well as a stage actor.

He is mostly known for his work as Gustav Graves in the James Bond film ‘Die Another Day, as Captain William Gordon in ‘Mangal Pandey: The Rising, Vincent McCarthy in ‘The Machine’ and Prince John of England in ‘Robin Hood.
