Brittany Mahomes Shares 'Perfect' Family Moment

Brittany Mahomes recently made some very wholesome Instagram posts about her family, showing what she called a "perfect" moment. She posted a photo of Patrick Mahomes and their children on her Instagram story, captioning it "The perfect Sunday."

Swifties Are Wary Of Brittany's Behavior

The tweets were, as readers can see, very mean, but in some of them, Brittany Mahomes was only 18, so it's completely possible that she has since changed her mind.

It's not just the tweets that angered swifties, though. Recently, Brittany collaborated with Kim Kardashian, and sources have called her out for being disloyal to Taylor Swift.
