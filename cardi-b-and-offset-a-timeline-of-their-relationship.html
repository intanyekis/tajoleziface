Cardi B and Offset's Relationship Timeline: Photos

Cardi B and Offset’s relationship has been a whirlwind with a secret wedding, a cheating scandal and two children together.

The rappers tied the knot in September 2017 after having their first date at Super Bowl LI in Houston in February of that year. They announced in July 2017 that they were expecting a baby and welcomed daughter Kulture in July 2018.

The twosome’s relationship was rocked, however, when videos surfaced that appeared to show Offset in bed with other women in December 2017 and January 2018. Though Cardi stayed with her man at the time, she announced in December 2018 that the couple had split.

Two months later, however, she had a change of heart, and the pair reconciled. In September 2020, Us Weekly confirmed that the “WAP” singer filed for divorce, and they split for a second time.
