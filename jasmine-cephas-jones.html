Jasmine Cephas Bio, Hamilton, Age, Net Worth, Instagram, Boyfriend

Jasmine Cephas who is also known as Jasmine Cephas Jones is an American actress. She got into fame after her appreciating role as Maria Reynolds and Margarita “Peggy” Schuyler Van Rensselaer in the award-winning musical Hamilton. Her 2018 movie “Blindspotting” got a lot of reviews and respect making the actress more popular. The actress is active since 2013 has now gained some fans and followers. Jasmine Cephas Birthday, Birthplace, Parents, and Education

The actress Cephas was born on 21st July 1989 in London, England. The actress was born to artistic parents Ron Cephas Jones and Kim Lesley. She spent most of her childhood in Brooklyn, New York. Her father being an actor and mother being a jazz singer was also taught to sing and dance at an early age. More information about her siblings and family are not provided.

Regarding her education, she was schooled at Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts where she learned a lot about music and acting skills. After she also attended Berklee College of Music for further progress in her art skills. At last, Jasmine got graduated from Neighborhood Playhouse School of the Theatre in 2011. During her time in high school, she used to participate in a lot of theaters and shows to enhance her acting skills.

Jasmine Cephas Boyfriend and Engagement

The struggling actress has not lived her life so openly in the eyes of the media. However, the gorgeous Jasmine is not single as of now. The 31-years old actress is in a happy relationship with an actor Anthony Ramos. Anthony is also a rising singer and a talented song-writer.

Jasmine Cephas Career and Movies

Jasmine Cephas began her career in 2013 with debut film “Titus”. The actress portrayed the role of Jessica in the movie and made her introduction to the film industry. Within a year, she again played the Co-lead role in the Matthieu O’Brien directed horror and thriller film “Fairfield”. In the movie, she played the role of Lindsey. The movie was released in 2014 and got much appreciation and support. With movies, she also pushed her role in series like “Blue Bloods” and “Unforgettable”.
