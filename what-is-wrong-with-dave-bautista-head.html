Who Is Dave Bautista?

David Michael Bautista Jr. is an American actor and retired professional wrestler who was born on January 18, 1969. Between 2002 and 2019, he had several stints in the WWE. Since the release of Guardians of the Galaxy in 2014, he has been best known for his role as Drax the Destroyer in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

Bautista started acting in 2006 and has been in The Man with the Iron Fists (2012), Riddick (2013), the James Bond movie Spectre (2015), Blade Runner 2049 (2017), Army of the Dead (2021), Dune (2021), and Glass Onion: A Knives Out Mystery (2021). Since 2009, he has also been in several direct-to-video movies.

Bautista signed a deal with Classic Entertainment & Sports in August 2012 to fight in Mixed Martial Arts (MMA). On October 6, 2012, he won his one and only Mma fight by knocking out Vince Lucero with a technical knockout in the first round.
