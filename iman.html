    Iman Facts

  • Prior to the age of 4, she had lived with her grandparents, after which she was sent off to boarding school in Egypt and attended schooling there throughout her adolescent and childhood years.
  • While Iman was studying at university, American photographer Peter Beard spotted her and had an influence in getting her to pursue modeling. After that, she relocated to the United States to start her career in the fashion industry.
  • She got her first modeling assignment in 1976, with Vogue.
  • Her home in New York was decorated by Jonathan Reed.
  • In 1994, Iman founded her brand Iman Cosmetics that is focused on making shades that are difficult to find for women. Later, in 2007, Iman formed Global Chic.
  • She is said to be a muse of well-known designers Gianni Versace, Thierry Mugler, Calvin Klein, Donna Karan, and is a favorite of Yves Saint Laurent.
  • Iman is multilingual and can speak Somali, Arabic, Italian, French, and English.
  • Over the years, Iman has worked with various well-known photographers such as Helmut Newton, Richard Avedon, Irving Penn, and Annie Leibovitz.
  • Her philanthropic work includes her work with several non-profit organizations such as CARE‘s first-ever Global Advocate, Keep a Child Alive, Children’s Defense Fund, Save the Children, and Enough Project.
