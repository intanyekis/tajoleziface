What Ons Jabeur Net Worth? How Rich Is The Tennis Player

Professional tennis player Ons Jabeur’s net worth is estimated at about 1.6 million dollars. To know more about her, keep reading the article.

Ons Jabeur is a Tunisina professional tennis player who has a career-high ranking of No. 24 by the Women’s Tennis Association (WTA). She is the current no. 1 ranked African player and the highest-ranked Arab player in the history of WTA.

On the ITF circuit, Ons has won eleven singles titles and one doubles title. Similarly, she has reached two WTA finals including one at the Premier-level 2018 Kremlin Cup in Russia. In 2019, she was named the Arab Woman of the Year in Sports.

Throughout her career, she has reached two junior Grand Slam girls’ singles finals at the French Open in 2010 and 2011, winning the title in the second appearance. She is now a mainstay on the WTA tour begging in 2017 but has a decade’s worth of experience playing at the ITF level.

At the 2021 Birmingham Classic, she won the first title of her career, thus becoming the first Arab and Tunisian woman tennis player to win a WTA title. Recently, on 30 June 2021, she defeated the five-time champion Venus Willimas in straight sets.
