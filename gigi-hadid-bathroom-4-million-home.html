Gigi Hadid Reveals Glimpse Of Colorful Room In $4 Million Home

Gigi Hadid typically keeps her private life away from the cameras. She has clearly defined the boundaries between the life she leads in the public sphere and the one that unfolds privately. As a result, when she does offer up a glimpse of her private space or details about her personal life, fans quickly take note and lap up the information they are gifted with. Gigi recently revealed a beautiful, bright space within her home, and it immediately created waves online. Hello Magazine reveals the stunning, vibrant bathroom in her very luxurious New York residence.

Supermodel Gigi Hadid is worth a jaw-dropping $29 million, and this continues to grow dramatically each year. She has graced some of the most highly esteemed runways, and alongside her supermodel sister Bella Hadid and her brother Anwar, is the heiress to the Hadid fortune. Known to live lavishly, Gigi owns several properties but resides primarily in her New York luxury condo.

She is sharing her space with her boyfriend, Zayn Malik, and their baby daughter, Khai. We hope they both like really bright colors, as it seems Gigi Hadid's home is a vibrant one. She has dyed pasta in all the different colors of the rainbow and has them on display as part of the storage units and cabinetry that they occupy. She has carried this bright, happy motif into her bathroom, which has millions of fans talking.

Her $4 million New York home has a bright bathroom that is completely lined with New Yorker Magazine covers in a variety of very bright colors.

Hello Magazine has described this bathroom as being 'psychedelic,' and went on to say that Gigi beamed with pride over this bathroom by captioning her bathroom video with the words, "used to dream about making this New Yorker powder room."

The bathroom looks to be a decent size but isn't as enormous as other celebrity bathrooms that have recently been revealed. The obsession with New Yorker magazines hasn't been completely uncovered, but one can assume this has a nostalgic connection to her personal life and upbringing.

The bathroom features marble elements and a wooden basin, and it is clearly a very bright, busy space.

When it comes to decorating, it seems bright color displays are Gigi's signature element.

This home boasts 2 bedrooms, one bathroom in addition to this rainbow one, and a stunning chef's kitchen.

While fans aren't sure they necessarily needed to see a glimpse of Gigi Hadid's toilet, the myriad of colors she uses to decorate her luxurious home is an interesting revelation that nobody expected to see, and they're excited to have been able to catch a glimpse.
