Rosamund Mary Ellen Pike’s Wiki Biography

Rosamund Mary Ellen Pike, born on 27th January 1979 in Hammersmith, London, UK, is known for her performance in the psychological thriller film “Gone Girl” (2014) and roles in films such as “Pride and Prejudice” (2005), “Die Another Day” (2002), and “An Education” (2009).
