Jey Uso Networth 2023, Earnings, Endorsements

American professional wrestler Joshua Fatu aka Jey Uso was born on August 22, 1985. He is a member of the fabled Samoan dynasty and goes by the stage name Jey Uso. He now appears on WWE Smackdown and is one half of the renowned tag team known as the Usos.

Together with his brother Jimmy Uso and Tamina, another Samoan wrestler, Jey Uso made his WWE debut on the main roster. Uso declared in his early years that he sought to get the respect of his family. He and his brother Jimmy Uso won the tag team championship in 2013, and since then, the Usos have been regarded as one of the finest tag teams in the company’s history.
