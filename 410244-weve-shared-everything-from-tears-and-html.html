'We've Shared Everything from Tears and Triumphs': Jane Seymour Shares TBT Photo with Her Look-Alike

Sally, Jane, and Anne Seymour | Source: Facebook/JaneSeymourOfficial | Instagram/janeseymour

Jayne Seymour dedicated much of her life to her career, but that didn't mean she forgot about family. The actress has a beautiful relationship with her sisters and often shares photos with them.

It has been decades since Jayne Seymour began her career in the entertainment industry. Through it all, she had her family's support and guidance.

She maintained her close relationship with her sisters, who all looked alike in their younger years. The "Dr. Quinn" star recently shared a throwback photo with her siblings, Sally and Anne, celebrating their experiences and strong bond.

"Life's journey is so much fuller and richer with my sisters by my side," she wrote. "We've shared everything from tears and triumphs." Although Jane and her sisters don't live within proximity, they are proud that their love "always bridges the gap."

The Hollywood star previously shared photos with them, one in 2018 and another in 2019. In one of her posts, Jane described her sisters as strong and beautiful.

In 2020, Jane and her sisters dressed as triplets to honor their late mother, like they sometimes would as children. The trio donned blue Everything But Water maxi dresses with colorful patterns on them while posing beside each other.

Although they weren't born in the same year, Jane, Sally, and Anne did look like triplets. In fact, their similarities extend beyond their appearance. In a previous interview, Jane revealed that they grew up doing the same things, wearing the same clothes, and enjoying the same experiences. She added:

"The only difference was that I was obsessed with ballet, and they weren't. So I went to professional ballet school, and they went to a different school."

Sally and Anne live in England; the former in Kew and the latter in Richmond. Since they have their residences there, Jane always has a comfortable place to stay when she visits.

Interestingly, Anne appeared in the film "Austenland" with her sister. The director immediately thought about casting Anne when she saw her photo. "Sharing screen time with her was a lot of fun," Jane revealed. "And she got really good reviews."

Jane has undoubtedly mastered her craft through the years, and working alongside her sister was a great experience. Besides her talent, Jane is known for preserving her beauty and figure despite her age. Jane Seymour often gets compliments from fans, who enjoy seeing her Instagram posts.
