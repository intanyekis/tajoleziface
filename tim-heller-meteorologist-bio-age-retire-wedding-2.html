Tim Heller Biography

He has served as the Chief Meteorologist at KTRK Channel 13, the ABC owned TV station in Houston, Texas for 16 years. Also, he is a Certified Broadcast Meteorologist and member of the American Meteorological Society. His weathercasts have also earned the Seal of Approval from the National Weather Association.

Tim Heller Education

He is a graduate of the Broadcast Meteorology Program at Mississippi State University. Actually, he has a BA in Communications from Clarke College in Dubuque, Iowa.

Tim Heller Career

His coverage of weather over the past three decades has earned him several awards, including four Emmy Awards. He has also received multiple Best Weathercast awards from the Houston Press Club, the Dallas Press Club, and the Texas Associated Press.

Before moving to Houston, he led the weather department at KDFW-TV in Dallas. He also worked at KGAN-TV in Cedar Rapids, Iowa, KRCG-TV in Jefferson City, Missouri, KDUB-TV in Dubuque, Iowa and WIFR-TV in Rockford, Illinois.

Tim Heller Retire

ABC13’s chief meteorologist, told the newsroom Tuesday that he has decided to retire from his career as a broadcast meteorologist and begin the second chapter in life. HOUSTON (KTRK) — Our fearless leader is leaving the television news business after nearly 35 years on the air. ABC13’s chief meteorologist last day on-air will be Friday, January 11, 2019.

REPLACEMENT

Weeks ago, the TV station announced its morning-side meteorologist Travis Herzog will take over Heller’s spot as a chief meteorologist, according to the station. David Tillman will be the station’s chief forecaster, with Elita Loresca taking over Herzog’s post on weekday mornings.
