Search and Retrieval Operations for Missing Free Diver

Efforts are currently underway to locate a 21-year-old college student and free diver who went missing while spearfishing in Santander town on Christmas Day. The search and retrieval operations are being conducted by the Philippine Coast Guard, the Municipal Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office of Santander, and other local government units. The authorities are working tirelessly to find the missing individual and bring him back to safety.

Missing Person Alert

A missing person alert was issued for a 21-year-old free diver who failed to return after going spearfishing in Liloan Dive Point, Santander. The young diver, identified as Louis Jullan Jugo Dayot, was reported missing on December 25. His disappearance has prompted a coordinated effort among local authorities and rescue teams to locate him and ensure his well-being.

Identification of the Missing Person

The missing person has been identified as Louis Jullan Jugo Dayot, a resident of San Sebastian in Samboan town. Dayot, a college student and experienced free diver, ventured out to Liloan Dive Point in Santander on Christmas morning to enjoy his favorite activity. Unfortunately, he did not return after a five-minute free dive, leading to concerns about his safety.

Current Status of the Search

As of the latest update, the 21-year-old Dayot remains missing. The local government of Samboan has released an advisory on social media, urging the public to provide any information that may assist in locating him. The search and retrieval operations are ongoing, with dedicated rescue personnel and volunteers tirelessly combing the area in hopes of finding the missing free diver.

Discovery of Scuba Gear in Amlan

A significant development in the search efforts occurred when scuba gear belonging to Dayot was found in Amlan town, located across Santander in Negros Oriental. This discovery has provided search teams with valuable clues and has expanded the search area. The authorities are carefully examining the scuba gear to gather any additional information that may aid in locating the missing diver.

Augmentation Forces from Argao and Guihulngan City

In a collaborative effort to enhance the search and retrieval operations, augmentation forces from the local governments of Argao and Guihulngan City in Negros Oriental are expected to join the ongoing efforts. Their expertise and resources will further strengthen the search teams’ capabilities, increasing the chances of finding Louis Jullan Jugo Dayot and bringing him back to his loved ones.

Details of the Incident

The incident involving the missing free diver has raised concerns and prompted a thorough investigation into the circumstances surrounding his disappearance. Authorities are working diligently to gather all available information and piece together the events that led to this unfortunate incident. Location and Activity

The missing free diver, Louis Jullan Jugo Dayot, was last seen at Liloan Dive Point in Santander, where he embarked on a spearfishing expedition with a friend. This picturesque location is known for its rich marine life and is a popular spot among diving enthusiasts. Dayot, an experienced free diver, was enjoying his passion for exploring the underwater world when the incident occurred.

Disappearance and Seeking Help

After successfully catching some fish, Dayot decided to venture deeper into the water for another dive. However, he failed to resurface after a five-minute dive, causing concern for his diving buddy. Realizing that something was amiss, the buddy immediately sought help from the authorities, initiating the search and rescue operations.
