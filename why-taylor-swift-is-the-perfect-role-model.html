3 Taylor Is Extremely Generous

Taylor understands the saying with great wealth comes great responsibility, which is why she often gives back to the community. It is understandable that she splurges on herself, but she also makes sure that she is giving enough back to the people. Over the course of her career, she has does numerous charity donations every year. Among her biggest donations were $1 million to the flood victims of Louisiana in 2016, donated all of her profits from the Welcome to New York track to the New York public schools in 2015, and donating all the proceeds of her Wildest Dreams video to the non-profit organization who preserves the national parklands in Africa, African Parks Foundation of America.
